About Me

Hands-on technical person with good amount of experience on designing, implementing scalable solutions. Have worked on microservices event driven architecture along with event sourcing patterns. Tend to have problem solving approach from business perspective.


Spent 13+ years in software industry, mainly working in banking area. Overall experience also include, working on public cloud platforms like AWS, where I took the ownership of delivering application using native AWS services. As part of the ownership also lies in implementing the CI/CD pipelines for deploying to production.


I got opportunity to design & deliver few things which made direct impact on creating business value for an FX Options Trading application. Designed service for notifying the external & internal customers with all trade information (AKA trade recaps) in real time. This trading app involves full suite from RFQs (via different interfaces), pricing, execution, booking, reporting etc. Also managed end to end release cycle involving many microservices and infrastructure. All micro services are deployed on OpenShift Cluster (Kubernetes).


As part of an application engaged in counterparty risk management, designed an interface (with Websocket exposure) which allows all internal bank client apps to connect to the newly developed (in-house) risk management system. Designed the whole load balancing approach for the module, making an ACTIVE-ACTIVE (HighAvailability) in different data center. This helped the bank in decomissioning vendor product and saved considerable amount in license cost.


Upgraded myself by making an exposure to machine learning concepts and have formal education in that field.
